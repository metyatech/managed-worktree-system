# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Contributors are expected to keep discussion professional, direct, and respectful.

Examples of expected behavior:

- focus criticism on ideas, code, and evidence
- make scope, tradeoffs, and uncertainty explicit
- respect different levels of experience
- assume good faith until evidence shows otherwise

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- personal attacks, harassment, or intimidation
- publishing private information without consent
- deliberately disruptive behavior
- bad-faith review or issue spam

## Enforcement

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the repository owner privately through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

Project maintainers may remove or edit comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.
